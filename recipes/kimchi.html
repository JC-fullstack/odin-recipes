Napa cabbage kimchi

Recipe type: cold, fermented, side dish, spicy

Ingredients: Asian chives, carrot, fermented salted shrimp, fish sauce, garlic, ginger, gochugaru, green onion, Korean radish, kosher salt, minari, napa cabbage, onion, sugar, sweet rice flour, Turbinado sugar, and water.

Ingredients

For Salting Cabbage:

  1. 6 pounds (about 2.7 kg) napa cabbage
  2. ½ cup Kosher salt (2.5 ounces: 72 grams)

For Making Porridge:

  1. 2 cups water
  2. 2 tablespoons sweet rice flour (aka glutinous rice flour)
  3. 2 tablespoons Turbinado sugar (or brown or white sugar)

Vegetables:

  1. 2 cups Korean radish matchsticks (or daikon radish)
  2. 1 cup carrot matchsticks
  3. 7 to 8 green onions (scallions), chopped
  4. 1 cup chopped Asian chives (buchu), optional (substitute with 3 green onions, chopped)
  5. 1 cup water dropwort (minari), optional

Seasonings and Spices:

  1. ½ cup garlic cloves (24 garlic cloves), minced
  2. 2 teaspoon ginger, minced
  3. 1 medium onion, minced
  4. ½ cup fish sauce
  5. ¼ cup fermented salted shrimp (saeujeot) with the salty brine, chopped
  6. 2 cups red pepper flakes (gochugaru)
